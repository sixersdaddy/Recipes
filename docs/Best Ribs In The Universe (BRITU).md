# Best Ribs In The Universe (BRITU)

**Course:** Any, BBQ rub
**Cuisine:** BBQ

**Drying Time:** 2-4 hrs
**Prep Time:** 30 min
**Total Time:** 2-4h 30m

**Servings:** 1-8 batches
**Calories:** unknown
**Author:** Mike Scrutchfield (metric / scaled version by Doc Walker)


## Ingredients

- Sugar In The Raw
- Kosher Salt (or other non-iodized salt)
- Brown Sugar, dried (full bag yields 8 batches)
- Chili Powder
- Ground Cumin
- Accent (MSG)
- Cayenne Pepper
- Black Pepper, freshly ground
- Granulated Garlic Powder
- Onion Powder

## Instructions

1. SPREAD brown sugar on large baking sheet and DRY in 250ºF oven for 2-4 hrs
2. MIX ingredients thoroughly using Cuisinart
3. STORE in airtight container for future use


## Notes

Sprinkle meat two hours before cooking with rub and allow meat to come to room temperature. Do not over season. A good overall dusting of the spices is all that’s needed. The spices will become a nice red, liquid coating after sitting for about an hour, if you used the proper amount.
