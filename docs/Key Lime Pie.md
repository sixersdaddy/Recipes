# Key Lime Pie 🍈

Delicious, easy-to-prepare key lime pie recipe

**Course:** Dessert  
**Cuisine:** American  

**Prep Time:** 15 min  
**Cook Time:** 17 min  
**Total Time:** 4 hrs  

**Servings:** 6-8  
**Calories:** unknown  
**Author:** unknown  


## Ingredients

Crust:
- 1-1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs (~1.5 packs or 200-210 g)
- 7 Tbsp butter - melted
- 3 Tbsp sugar

Filling:
- Key limes (enough for 3/4 cups juice or substitute key lime juice)
- 2 x 14 oz cans sweetened condensed milk
- 1/2 cup light sour cream
- 1 Tbsp zest (about 4 key limes or 2 limes)


## Instructions

1. COMBINE crust ingredients using a fork and PRESS FIRMLY into pie pan (ANY SIZE - you will have thicker crust if using a smaller pie pan)
2. BAKE at 375°F for 7 minutes; don't allow to brown
3. COOL for at least 30 minutes before adding filling
4. ZEST key limes; make sure to only get the green part of the skin
5. Gently FOLD the sour cream into the condensed milk with a whisk and ADD the juice until blended. ADD zest and BLEND.
6. When the crust has cooled to room temperature, ADD the filling and more zest to the top
7. BAKE at 350°F for 10 minutes
8. COOL to room temperature and then REFRIGERATE for 3 hours
9. Decorate with lime slice(s)
10. OPTIONAL - ADD whipped cream to cover the surface and garnish with lime
