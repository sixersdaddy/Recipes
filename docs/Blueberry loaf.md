# Blueberry Loaf

## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup milk
- 1/3 cup melted butter (6 Tablespoons)
- 1 large egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 cups all purpose flour (8 oz)
- 1 cup sugar (8 oz)
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt (1 x 0.5 teaspoons)
- 2-1/2 teaspoons baking powder (5 x 0.5 teaspoons)
- 2 cups fresh blueberries (slightly less than 1 pint package)

Crumb Topping:

- 1/2 cup sugar (4 oz)
- 1/4 cup all purpose flour (1 oz)
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/8 cup melted butter (2 Tablespoons)

### Add ingredients to bread maker

- Install kneading paddle
- Add liquid ingredients first, followed by dry ingredients
- Use #7 Cake/Quick cycle (timer should start at 1:38)
- Periodically scrape flour from sides of bread pan with a rubber spatula
- Mix crumb topping together (add butter just before use) with fork until uniform; set aside until bread has 1 hour left to cook

### When kneading completes (1:30 remaining)

- Remove kneading paddle and scrape batter with rubber spatula
- Fold the blueberries into the batter, especially edges

### One Hour Remaining (1:00 remaining)

- Add crumb topping

### When cooking completes

- Leave bread pan in bread maker until cool
- Remove bread pan and place upside down on rack; slice while loaf upside down


## Notes

- Use all purpose flour; do not use self rising or bread flour (SR flour rises too much and isn't designed for bread makers)
- Use refined granulated sugar for crumb topping; do not use Sugar In The Raw (SITR is too clumpy and doesn't distribute well)
