# Sweet Bread

Perfect dinner bread to complement pulled pork or brisket BBQ.

## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup milk
- 1/3 cup melted butter (6 Tablespoons)
- 1 large egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 cups all purpose flour (8 oz)
- 1 cup sugar (8 oz)
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt (1 x 0.5 teaspoons)
- 2-1/2 teaspoons baking powder (5 x 0.5 teaspoons)

### Add ingredients to bread maker

- Install kneading paddle
- Add liquid ingredients first, followed by dry ingredients
- Use #7 Cake/Quick cycle (timer should start at 1:38)
- Periodically scrape flour from sides of bread pan with a rubber spatula

### When kneading completes (1:30 remaining)

- Remove kneading paddle and scrape batter with rubber spatula

### When cooking completes

- Remove bread pan and allow to cool on a rack for 1 hour before slicing


## Notes

- Use all purpose flour; do not use self rising or bread flour (SR flour rises too much and isn't designed for bread makers)
