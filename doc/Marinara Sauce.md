# Marinara Sauce

Authentic Italian marinara sauce from scratch.

**Course:** dinner, pasta
**Cuisine:** Italian

**Prep Time:** 10 min
**Cook Time:** 30-60 min
**Total Time:** 40-70 min

**Servings:** 4
**Calories:** 89 kcal
**Author:** Melissa / keepingitsimpleitalian.com


## Ingredients

- 24.5 oz tomato purée (ref. Solo Pomodoro / MUTTI brand)
- 4 fresh basil leaves - rough chopped
- 1/4 cup yellow onion - finely chopped
- 4 cloves garlic - finely minced
- 2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tsp Kosher salt - to taste
- 1/8 tsp dried oregano
- 2 Tbsp tomato paste (optional) - use if sauce doesn’t thicken enough after simmering


## Instructions

1. ADD olive oil to a medium sauce pan over medium heat
2. ADD onion and COOK until lightly golden and translucent, about 4 minutes
3. ADD garlic and COOK for an additional minute
4. ADD tomato purée, basil leaves, salt, & oregano, and STIR well
5. SIMMER covered for 30+ minutes, STIRRING occasionally
6. OPTIONAL - ADD tomato paste if thicker sauce is desired
7. SERVE tossed with your favorite pasta


## Notes

**Storing:** Refrigerate up to a week in a container fitted with a lid.
**Canning:** Refer to separate canning instructions (7 pint jars, 10 psi, 25 min)
